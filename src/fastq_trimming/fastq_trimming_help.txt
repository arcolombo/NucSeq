Description:
  Trim reads in a FASTQ file.

Usage:
	fastq_trimming.sh  [OPTIONS]  -- FASTQ_FILE

Options:
  -h|--help           Help
  -d|--outdir         Output directory
  -l|--readLength     Final read length. Default: '50'

Examples:               
  fastq_trimming.sh -l 40 -- testRound1_R1.fastq.gz
