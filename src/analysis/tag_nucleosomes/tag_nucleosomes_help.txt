Description:
    Tags nucleosomes and expands them. 

Usage:
    tag_nucleosomes.sh  [options]  -- PEAKS_FILE SMOOTH_FILE

Options:
    -h|--help           Help.
    -d|--outdir         Output directory. Default: $PWD.
    -t|--threads        Threads used. Default: 2.

Examples:
    tag_nucleosomes.sh -d out -t 8 -- r4s1_peaks.cff.gz r4s1_smooth.cff.gz

Dependancies:
    NA

Upstream:
    gaussian_kernel.sh
    peak_calling.sh
