Description:
  Sort a BAM file. 

Usage:
  bam_sort.sh  [OPTIONS]  -- BAM_FILE

Options:
  -h--help                Help.
  -o--outdir              Output directory. Default: $PWD

Examples:
  bam_sort.sh -t 16 -m 100M -- paired_end.bam 

Dependancies:
  samtools
